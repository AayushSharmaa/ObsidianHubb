## Features

- ### Light & Dark Theme
  Enjoy the flexibility of choosing between light & dark themes to suit your preferences and reduce eye strain.

#### Dark Mode

![Dark Mode](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zaheralmajed/vicious-theme-obsidian/main/assets/dark.png)

#### Light Mode

![Dark Mode](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zaheralmajed/vicious-theme-obsidian/main/assets/light.png)

- ### Modern Card Style
  Experience a sleek and contemporary card design that enhances visual appeal and improves user experience.

![Cards](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zaheralmajed/vicious-theme-obsidian/main/assets/card.png)
